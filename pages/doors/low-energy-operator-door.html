Safe & ADA-Compliant Low Energy Door Operators

Low Energy Operators provide a safe and reliable automatic access solution for commercial and institutional environments. Primarily applied to new or existing swing doors, these systems can be activated through push plates, motion sensors, or access control systems. Their operation with reduced speed and force strictly complies with ADA requirements and ANSI/BHMA standards, ensuring effortless accessibility and safety for all occupants.
